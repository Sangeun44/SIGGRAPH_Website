Our Chapter

Penn's SIGGRAPH Chapter is a computer graphics oriented group which focuses on fostering a community of students interested in animation, computer science, games and VFX. Some of SIGGRAPH's activities include a Demo Reel Night, weekly talks on computer graphics topics, tutorials, and movie nights.
